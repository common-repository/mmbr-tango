MMBR™ helps you earn revenue from your website and content: live events, podcasts, webinars, blogs, videos and more. Visitors can make one-time and subscription purchases to access your protected content.

== Description ==

MMBR is a simple plugin that allows any website owner or content publisher to easily sell access to any type of content, whether it’s a blog post, live events, podcasts, webinars, videos. We make it easy to charge for access to your content without making any changes to your website.

You just create a MMBR account at <https://dashboard.mmbr.io/register>, fill in your site details, download our plugin and feed your MMBR site key to the plugin. Once this is completed, you can start accepting credit card payments for your content. We take care of the details involved.

Your visitors can feel safe knowing transaction are processed in a secure environment. VISA, MasterCard, Amex, Debit payments are allowed.

Visit <www.mmbr.io> for more!

== Installation ==

1. In your WordPress dashboard, go to Plugins->Add New and search for MMBR in WordPress plugins, then simply click “install”.
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress.
3. Sign into <https://dashboard.mmbr.io>, create a site, declare your protected pages and retrieve your site public key.
4. Back at your Wordpress > MMBR setting, type in the site public key and click "Use this key"

= What is MMBR? =

MMBR helps you earn revenue from your website content:live events, podcasts, webinars, blogs, videos and more. With an easy to install paywall, we help you focus on publishing content. Your visitors improve your publicity and everyone gets paid.

= How much does MMBR cost? =

MMBR takes a small fee from each transaction, if you choose to charge for access to your MMBR Tango protected content. You set the subscription price and your visitors can begin subscribing immediately. Our transaction fee is between 2.9% and 5.9% depending on the amount of revenue your subscriptions generate. The more revenue generated by your subscriptions, the lower the MMBR transaction fee is.

2.9%* + $0.30

Includes all Credit Card (mechant) processing fees
Includes daily bank transfers

No escrow period, No monthly fees, No setup fees

* The transaction variable rate default is 5.9% + $0.35 and decreases as annual revenue increases. The best rate is 2.9% + $0.30 @ $500K annually. More information available at <http://www.mmbr.io/pricing/>
